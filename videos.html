Video 1: Stick Handling Drills

Here's a great stick handling video with many different stick handling drills you can easily do in the basement, garage or outside. Try and use a stick handling ball or a golf ball, those two will work better for the drills. Once you get these drills down, try challenging yourself to do them with your head up and on a swivel.

Video 2: Drills to Improve your Snapshot

These shooting drills will help you improve your shot for next season. They can be done off the ice and will help strengthen your forearms. Make sure when you are shooting your hands are off your body and you are in an athletic position so you won’t be knocked off the puck. You can also add in curl and drag shooting around an obstacle.

Video 3: Great Speed Workouts

This video has many great speed workouts. This will help with speed, agility and quickness on the ice. Really focus on sticking the landing before doing your next jump. You won't benefit from these workouts if you don't do them right. Once you are doing these workouts really well, pick up the speed and pace, push yourself, that is how you get better!

Video 4: More Stickhandling with Footwork

These drills will help you to get your feet moving while stick handling. Just like when on the ice, the best players are able to move their feet while handling a puck and scanning the ice. Try this slow to get your rhythm down. Then you will be able to speed it up while scanning what’s in front of you.
